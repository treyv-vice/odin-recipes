Garden Chili

Ingredients

  • 1x 5qt crockpot
  • 2 lbs Hamburger
  • 1.5x Yellow onions
  • 2x Green bell peppers
  • 2x 14.5oz can kidney beans
  • 1x 14.5oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2x 10oz can Rotel (or similar brand) diced toamtoes and chopped green chilies
  • Chili powder
  • Cholula original hot sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • 64oz tomato juice

Steps

  1. Brown all of the hambuger and drain any remaining grease.
  2. While hamburger cooks, chop both bell peppers and the onions and set aside.
  3. Add hamburger to crockpot, followed by chopped bell peppers and onions.
  4. Open the cans of kidney beans and drain juice. Add the beans to the crockpot.
  5. Open the cans of diced tomatoes and tomatoes and green chilies. Add these all to the crockpot.
  6. Add 3tbsp chili powder and 1 tbsp pepper.
  7. Add all 64oz of tomato juice and 4 tbsp Cholula hot sauche, and stir the crockpot.
  8. Turn heat to high until simmering. Then set heat to low and let simmer for 4 hours, stirring occasionally.
  9. Add more chili powder, pepper, and Cholula to taste and serve hot with crackers and shredded cheese!
